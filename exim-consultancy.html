Exim Consultancy

SEAWAVE FORWARDING AND LOGISTICS PVT LTD provides consultancy relating to law, act, provisions, circulars, notifications, etc., of Customs, GST and allied departments to clients. Providing actionable insights within Customs, GST and Allied departments, we help clients foresee growth opportunities.

Shipments for export and import with complete operational support

In addition to providing back-office support for your operations, we also safeguard your payment risks

Pre-Shipment:

  • Quotation preparation (including cost sheet).
  • Working with the buyer or importer to determine payment and delivery terms.
  • Selection of port.
  • Preparation of Proforma Invoice.
  • Verification of draft L/C to be obtained from the customer.
  • Obtain PO / Issue PO to the customer / Vendors.

Post-Shipment:

  • A preparation process for commercial invoices.
  • Preparation of the packing list.
  • In all documents, make sure the correct payment terms are stated.
  • Pre-shipment inspection certificates (if any).
  • Customs documentation.
  • Submission of documentation for payment collection to the bank.
